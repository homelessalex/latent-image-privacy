Privacy Policy

Latent Image · Last updated 17 June 2026

Latent Image does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no network connectivity, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

Camera

Camera access is used solely to capture photos. All capture and image processing happens entirely on your device.

Photos

Photos you take are saved to your own device Photo Library. The app never uploads or transmits them anywhere.

Settings & styles

Your settings and editing styles are stored locally on your device only.

Data sharing

Because no data ever leaves your device, we do not collect any information about you and have nothing to share with third parties.
